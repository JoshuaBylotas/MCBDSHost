## ? FAQ

**Q: How long until my site ranks?**
A: Google: 1-7 days indexing, then 1-3 months for good rankings. Bing: 1-2 weeks indexing.

**Q: Do I need to submit to all search engines?**
A: No. Google (90%) + Bing (5%) = 95% of traffic. Focus on these two first.

**Q: Why is DuckDuckGo automatic?**
A: DuckDuckGo uses Bing's index, so submitting to Bing automatically covers DuckDuckGo.

**Q: Should I submit to Yandex or Baidu?**
A: Only if you're specifically targeting those regions (Russia/CIS or China).

**Q: How do I verify my submission worked?**
A: Search `site:mcbdshost.com` in Google and Bing. Pages should appear within 1-2 weeks.

**Q: What if I get indexing errors?**
A: Refer to the detailed guide for troubleshooting. Most errors are easy to fix.

**Q: How often should I resubmit my sitemap?**
A: Only when you add new pages. Google auto-crawls daily without resubmission.

**Q: Can I speed up indexing?**
A: Yes. Use "Request indexing" in Google Search Console for faster crawling.
