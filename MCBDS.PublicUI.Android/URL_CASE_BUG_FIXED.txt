=== URL CASE SENSITIVITY BUG - FIXED ===

THE PROBLEM:
When you typed: Https://WINSERVER03.bylotas.net:8081 (capital H)
The app created: http://Https://WINSERVER03.bylotas.net:8081 (DOUBLE PROTOCOL!)

WHY IT HAPPENED:
The NormalizeUrl function checked for "https://" (lowercase only)
Since "Https://" != "https://", it thought there was no protocol
So it added "http://" prefix, resulting in "http://Https://..."

THE FIX:
Changed all protocol checks to be case-INSENSITIVE:
- url.IndexOf("://", StringComparison.OrdinalIgnoreCase)
- url.StartsWith("http://", StringComparison.OrdinalIgnoreCase)
- url.StartsWith("https://", StringComparison.OrdinalIgnoreCase)

NOW IT HANDLES:
? https://server.com ? https://server.com
? Https://server.com ? https://server.com (normalized to lowercase)
? HTTPS://server.com ? https://server.com (normalized to lowercase)
? hTtPs://server.com ? https://server.com (normalized to lowercase)
? hpps://server.com ? https://server.com (typo fixed)
? server.com ? http://server.com (default protocol added)
